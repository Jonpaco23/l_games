#Below are all of the trigger codes for the EEG Experiment.

0: The experiment has begun/first movement

1: The robot is moving left inward

2: Left target has been hit

3: The robot has gone through the middle

4: Right target has been hit

5: Down target has been hit

6: Up target has been hit

7: New target has appeared

8: The experiment has ended

9: Robot is moving left outward

10. Robot is moving down inward

11. Robot is moving down outward

12. Robot is moving right inward

13. Robot is moving right outward

14. Robot is moving up inward

15. Robot is moving up outward

16. Break has begun

17. Break has ended
